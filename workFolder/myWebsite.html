Optical Illusions

This website contains some cool optical illusions!

Here are 3 well known optical illusions

  • Müller-Lyer illusion
  • Ebbinghaus illusion
  • Hermann Grid Illusion

Here is more information about each one:

Müller-Lyer illusion Two lines of the same length appear to be different sizes due to arrowheads.
Ebbinghaus Illusion Two circles of the same size appear to be differnet sizes due to surrounding circles
Hermann Grid Illusion White intersecting lines appear to have a black dot at the intersection.
